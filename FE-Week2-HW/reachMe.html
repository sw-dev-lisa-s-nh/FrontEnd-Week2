Resource for Cello Music

IMSLP, also known as the International Music Score
Library Project or Petrucci Music Library, was started
in 2006. The logo on the main page is a capital letter A.
It was taken from the beginning of the very first printed
book of music, the Harmonice Musices Odhecaton. It was
published in Venice in 1501 by Ottaviano Petrucci, the
library's namesake.
